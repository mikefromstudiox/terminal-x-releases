# Terminal X — Marketing & Promotion Plan

**Product:** Terminal X POS (terminalxpos.com)
**Company:** Terminal X SRL
**Market:** Dominican Republic (Santo Domingo, Santiago)
**Updated:** 2026-04-13
**Pricing:** Pro RD$2,490/mo | Pro PLUS RD$4,490/mo | Pro MAX RD$6,990/mo (annual 15% OFF)

---

## Selling Points

1. **100% working e-CF** (electronic fiscal receipts) with QR codes — certified Emisor Electronico per Ley 32-23
2. **May 15 2026 DGII deadline** creates urgency — every business in DR needs this NOW
3. **Full POS + payroll + inventory + commissions + reports** in one system
4. **Desktop + Web** — works offline, syncs to cloud
5. **WhatsApp receipts** — customers get receipts via WhatsApp (Pro MAX)
6. **No intermediary fees** — businesses using ef2.do or similar pay RD$50-100K+/mo. Terminal X eliminates that
7. **Built for DR** — Spanish UI, RD$ currency, DGII 606/607 reports, TSS/ISR payroll calculations
